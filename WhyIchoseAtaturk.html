Why I Chose Ataturk

Father of Turkey

By Oguz Sarac

I chose Ataturk because he is a founder of the Republic of Turkey. The Parliament granted him the surname Ataturk, which means "Father of Turks". I know that without Ataturk, Turkey would be an insignificant country in the Middle East, fighting extremism and civil unrest like the rest of the Middle East.

He loved children. I know that when he has free time he always trying to spend time with them. He presented them with a festival which is unique in World. Every year on the 23rd April, children from all over around the World are come to Turkey to celebrate 'The National Sovereignty and The Children Festival'.

I can walk freely in my country, I can go to the school and study which subject I want as other students in the World do. I can wear whatever I want, I am totally free just because of Ataturk. So I am grateful to him for everything he did.
